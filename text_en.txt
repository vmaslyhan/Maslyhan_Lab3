Nowadays, digital technologies penetrate into all spheres of human life, changing the usual processes of work, education and entertainment.Modern devices, in particular tablets and smartphones, combine many functions: they serve as communicators, photo and video cameras, organizers and even navigators.Thanks to the global Internet, getting the necessary information has become much easier, and online communities have transformed communication and maintaining contacts to a new level.At the same time, technological progress creates certain risks: long-term use of gadgets can affect health, cause eye fatigue, sleep disturbances and psychological dependence.
